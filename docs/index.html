03 The scoring engine

Not a vibe check.
A measurable contract.

01

Generate

Every scenario creates repeatable alerts, metrics, logs, traces, runbooks, noise, and hidden truth from a fixed seed.

02

Investigate

The candidate receives the same evidence an operator would inspect. Misleading but plausible clues are included on purpose.

03

Score

Terms and evidence signals map to explicit weights. Blaming a red herring removes points. No opaque evaluator decides the outcome.

04

Improve

Missing signals and remediation terms turn a single score into a useful explanation of what the RCA failed to prove.
